Car Show Project: Purpose, Audience, and Goal

Purpose:

To create a centralized online hub for the Old Town Car Show that provides clear, accessible information about the event.

Audience:

  • Car enthusiasts and collectors who value classic, custom, and hot rod culture.
  • Individuals with a strong appreciation for traditional car values and craftsmanship.

Goal:

  • Deliver all event information in one location:
  • Expectations for attendees
  • Event details and schedules
  • Vendor information
  • Car registration requirements
  • Ensure the experience reflects the community values while being informative, professional, and easy to navigate.

Name:Car Show Enthusiast - Attendee/Vendors/Sponsors/Participant

12th year

Background:

  • Attends local car shows regularly, enjoys family-friendly events.
  • Interested in checking vehicle entry requirements.
  • Comes with family/friends, might not be tech-savvy.

Technology Knowledge:

  • Uses a phone first (mobile-friendly) design is critical.
  • Looks for quick links:event date/time, location/direction, registration forms, vendor sign-up, sponsor information.
  • Wants easy-to-read maps and schedules without too many clicks.
  • Comfortable with basic browsing, filling out forms, and moble apps/maps.
  • Not always familiar with downloading PDFs or navigating complex menus

Attitude & Behavior:

  • Excited about the event but impatient with confusing layouts.
  • Skims pages quickly to find what's relevant to them (time, place, cost).

Frustrations & Needs:

  • Gets frustrated if information is hidden or buried in text-heavy pages.
  • Needs clear instruction for car entry registration and vendors applications.
  • Wants reassurance about family-friendly amenities (park, bathroom, food, kid activities.

Learning Goals (Full Stack Course):

  • Learn how to register a car online.
  • Understanding what is allowed (vehicle requirements, what vendors can sell, sponsorship tiers.
  • Figure out what's new this year compared to past shows.
